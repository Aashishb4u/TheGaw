Features

  • Features an integrated RFID chip for efficient asset tracking and real-time data collection.
  • 360° x 185° swivel or 360° x 360° swivel allows the wrench to operate with no space limitation.
  • The piston rod of the torque wrench boasts hard chrome plating, enhancing wear resistance and ensuring long-lasting performance.
  • The reaction pawl officially overcomes the possibility of ratchet reversing opposite; enhancing the efficiency and precision of torque.
  • A compact design with a longer arm fully advances reliability.
  • Operator Safety, Low Clearance, Interchangeable & Cross Compatible Design.
