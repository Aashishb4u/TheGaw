Gravity Unloading Flow Meter

The portable flowmeter is designed for gravity flow unloading of tankers at gas stations and pipeline measurement, handling various oil products (excluding water). Its versatile design ensures simple, flexible installation and operation, making it easy to use across a range of settings. Equipped with Bluetooth connectivity and historical data tracking, it enables real-time monitoring and analysis. With high precision, reliable performance, and a long service life, it connects directly to tank outlets without requiring a pump, offering seamless and efficient operation.

Features

  • The flowmeter offers high accuracy with ±0.2% precision, featuring 3 flow sensors and 1 temperature sensor for accurate measurements.
  • It utilizes advanced temperature compensation with V20 and V15 double compensation to ensure stable performance.
  • The meter includes a patent flow measurement calculation formula, guaranteeing accurate flow measurement.
  • Device calibration allows for the calibration of multiple points, ensuring consistent measurement integrity.
  • It features dual protection with password and seal lock mechanism for enhanced security.
  • The flowmeter supports Bluetooth and app connectivity.
  • The app is compatible with Windows, Android, iOS, offering export options for reports in Excel or PDF formats.
  • Certified with patent, CE, MID, and ATEX for safety and compliance.
  • Equipped with an automatic pressure relief valve for operational safety.
  • LCD display shows product type, temperature, flow volume, and flow speed for easy monitoring.
