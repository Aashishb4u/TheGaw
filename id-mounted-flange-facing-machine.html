ID Mounted Flange Facing Machine

TheGAW ID Mounted Flange Facing Machine features a three-piece mounting system, making setup and alignment quick, simple, and hassle-free. This portable, lightweight, and powerful machine is ideal for facing, bevelling, and turning pipe, valve, and pump flanges with precision and ease. Designed with operator comfort and environmental concerns in mind, the machine operates quietly, significantly reducing noise pollution in the workplace.

Features

  • Equipped with an RFID chip for easy and real-time tracking, and maintenance history.
  • Easy installation and self-centering id mounted mechanical swelling.
  • Three axis servo feed system to process the complex end bevel.
  • Designed for bevelling and facing of heavy-duty pipes and flanges.
  • Powered with a swivel tool post for grooves, RTJ Flanges and bevels.
  • Safe, reliable and continuous groove-facing feeds.
