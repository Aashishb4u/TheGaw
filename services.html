TheGAW Production Services

TheGAW supplies a comprehensive range of advanced controlled bolting solutions for Oil & Gas, Renewable, Mining, Heavy, and Structural industries. Through our continuous improvement and our commitment to cater to our customers with environment-friendly solutions, we deliver cutting-edge technology products to achieve operational excellence without any accidents and to achieve a step closer to industry 4.0. We provide the safest, advanced, and productive bolting solutions to ensure complete joint integrity, reduced human, and operation risks. TheGAW bolting solutions consist of hydraulic, pneumatic, pumps, battery tools, joint Integrity Software, bolt tensioners, and much more.
