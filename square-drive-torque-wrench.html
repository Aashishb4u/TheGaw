Square Drive Torque Wrench

TheGAW Square Drive Hydraulic Torque Wrenches boast a compact, high-strength uni-body construction, ensuring durability and easy access in confined spaces. These tools deliver precise torque values for uniform bolt tightening, made from high-strength, corrosion-resistant materials. Designed for longevity, they ensure safety and optimal performance even in the most demanding environments.
