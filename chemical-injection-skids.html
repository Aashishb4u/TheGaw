Applications

  • Oil and Gas
    • Corrosion Inhibition: Prevents corrosion in pipelines, wellheads, and equipment.
    • Scale Prevention: Injects scale inhibitors to maintain pipeline efficiency.
    • Hydrate Management: Delivers chemicals to prevent hydrate formation in gas pipelines.
    • Demulsification: Separates water and oil in production streams.
  • Petrochemical and Refining
    • Process Optimization: Adds catalysts or inhibitors to optimize chemical reactions.
    • Corrosion Protection: Protects storage tanks, pipelines, and processing equipment.
  • Marine and Offshore
    • Marine Growth Prevention: Prevents biofouling in offshore platforms and pipelines.
    • Flow Assurance: Maintains the integrity of flow lines through chemical injection.
