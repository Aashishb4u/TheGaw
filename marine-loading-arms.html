Marine Loading Arms

TheGAW's marine loading arms are expertly engineered for the efficient transfer of liquids and gases to and from ships, barges, and LNG vessels. Manufactured to meet the OCIMF (Oil Companies International Marine Forum) Design & Construction Specificationss, our loading arms incorporate advanced manual and hydraulic controls, delivering unmatched safety and reliability in even the most challenging marine environments.
