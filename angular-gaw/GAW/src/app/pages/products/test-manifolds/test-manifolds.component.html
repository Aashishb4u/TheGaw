Hydrotest Manifold & Air Manifold

TheGAW Hydrotest and Air Manifolds are engineered to deliver high-performance solutions for pressure testing and compressed air distribution in the oil and gas industry. Designed with safety, durability, and efficiency, these manifolds are built to meet the highest standards, ensuring reliability in critical applications.

Features

  • Customizable options for pressure ratings, outlet configurations, and materials for corrosive environments.
  • Designed to handle pressures up to 20,000 PSI, ideal for high-pressure testing.
  • Made from premium stainless steel or carbon steel for superior corrosion resistance and durability.
  • Equipped with calibrated pressure gauges, leak-proof valves, and fittings for reliable performance.
  • Pressure relief valves and ergonomic controls ensure safety and reduce operator errors.
  • Complies with ASME standards for safety, construction, and flange connection compatibility.

Benefits

  • After-sale technical support.
  • Reduce maintenance and replacement costs.
  • Reliable monitoring and leak-proof performance ensure accurate pressure testing.
  • Modular design offers flexibility for diverse applications and configurations.
  • ASME compliance ensures adherence to safety and regulatory standards.

Applications

  • Oil & Gas and Petrochemicals: Critical for pipeline, wellhead, and process equipment.
  • Power Generation: Ensure the integrity of steam line, turbine, and condenser performance.
  • Manufacturing: Tests valves, pressure vessels, and industrial components.
  • Mining: Confirms the reliability of hydraulic systems in heavy machinery.
  • Aerospace: Ensures that fuel tanks and related components can withstand high-pressure scenarios.
