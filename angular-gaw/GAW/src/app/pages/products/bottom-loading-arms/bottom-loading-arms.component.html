Bottom Loading Arms

Bottom loading arms are designed to improve operator safety by allowing fluid transfer from ground level. These arms are particularly useful for road and rail tankers, providing faster, safer, and more efficient loading operations. TheGAW’s bottom loading arms can be customized to include couplings for vapor return, anti-static features, and breakaway valves to prevent fluid loss and ensure environmental sustainability.
