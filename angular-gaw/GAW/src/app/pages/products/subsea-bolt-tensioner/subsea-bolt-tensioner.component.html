Subsea Bolt Tensioner

TheGAW Our Subsea Bolt Tensioners are engineered for exceptional performance in demanding underwater environments. The compact, ROV-friendly design enables efficient deep-water operations. Featuring advanced sealing technology and pressure-compensated systems, these tools deliver precise tensioning while preventing hydraulic fluid contamination, making them ideal for critical subsea bolting operations.

Features

  • Integrated RFID technology provides enhanced traceability and real-time performance monitoring in subsea operations.
  • The ASST series subsea tensioner is designed with a quick-releasing nut for operational trustworthiness and improved working.
  • Two rotation ways: manually or with Tommy bar.
  • Easy operation with anti-skid function.
  • High-strength anticorrosion, prolonging service life.
  • Manufactured subsea bolt tensioners from high-quality materials with an ergonomic design, making it easier for the operator/diver to use continuously in challenging subsea environments.
  • Subsea bolt tensioners incorporating the quick reaction nut feature reduces diver fatigue, improving diver safety and productivity.
