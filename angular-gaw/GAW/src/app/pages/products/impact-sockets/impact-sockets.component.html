Impact Sockets

TheGAW Impact Sockets are manufactured from high-strength chrome molybdenum steel, ensuring durability and performance under heavy impact loads. Designed for optimal strength and access in confined spaces, these sockets accommodate a wide range of fasteners, including standard, deep, and thin-wall variations. A comprehensive selection of sizes and accessories, including long length, bi-hexagonal, and specialized sockets, is available to meet diverse application needs.

Features

  • Standard, deep, and thin wall sockets.
  • The depth of the hex openings is designed to accommodate the full standard fastener head.
  • Up to 2 1/2" square drive Impact Sockets through to 195mm (7.1/4") across the flats.
  • Long length, bi-hexagonal and special sockets are available on request.
  • TheGAW Special Sockets include Magnetic socket & Smart Sockets with Bluetooth embedded.
