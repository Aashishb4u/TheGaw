Manual Flange Facing Machine

TheGAW Manual Flange Machine is designed for efficient manual flange surface repairs by installing the tool on the inner wall of the flange using an internal clamping mechanism. It operates seamlessly in locations without power or gas supply, and its lightweight design simplifies operation, on-site installation, and disassembly, significantly lowering maintenance costs.

Features

  • Equipped with an RFID chip for streamlined asset management, real-time tracking, and maintenance history.
  • Operates without requiring electricity or gas.
  • Lightweight design crafted from high-strength materials ensures ease of handling and durability.
  • Adjustable tool holder with internal clamping mechanism accommodates flange diameters from 25 mm to 350 mm.
  • Precision centering supported by the inner flange wall guarantees flawless alignment.
  • Replaceable lead screw enables optimal feed rate, suitable for mirror-finish and water-grain line processing.
