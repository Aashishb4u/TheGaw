Spring Return Bolt Tensioner

TheGAW Spring Return Bolt Tensioners are designed with a spring-assisted hydraulic piston, ensuring faster bolt tensioning cycles and enhanced efficiency. Their ergonomic design, featuring an anti-slip surface, provides ease of use and improved operator safety. Compatible with a wide range of flanges and bolting systems, these tensioners are ideal for demanding oil and gas applications. Built to withstand high working pressures, they deliver precision, adaptability, and reliability in various industrial settings.

Features

  • Embedded RFID technology ensures accurate tracking and efficient service history management.
  • Designed to fit BS/ANSI/API flanges.
  • Automatic spring-return piston retraction.
  • Max tool pressure – 21,750 psi (1,500 bar working pressure).
  • Spring-assisted return hydraulic piston reduces bolt tensioning cycle times considerably.
  • Models cover ranges from 3/4”, 4 1/2” & M20 – M145 bolts, making them suitable for a wide range of flange sizes.
  • Spring Return Tensioners allow greater efficiency compared to manual return tensioners.
