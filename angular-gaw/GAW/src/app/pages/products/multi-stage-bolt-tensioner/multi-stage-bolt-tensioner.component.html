Multi Stage Bolt Tensioner

TheGAW Multi-Stage Bolt Tensioners are designed for high-load applications in confined spaces, delivering exceptional precision and reliability. Ideal for critical bolting tasks, wind turbine installations, and slewing rings, they offer fast, accurate, and efficient tightening. These tools are engineered for long-life performance with minimal maintenance, featuring advanced seal technology and over-stroke protection. Built to withstand demanding environments, they ensure uninterrupted operations and are readily available for quick delivery.

Features

  • The RFID chip enables precise tool tracking, ensuring efficient asset maintenance.

  • Optimized for use on wind turbines, designed for long-life, low-maintenance use, and suitable for use on single bolts or grouped together for multiple tightening of bolted joints.
  • High cycle life, over-stroke protection, and the latest seal technology ensure low-maintenance operation to keep projects on schedule.
