Nut Splitter

TheGAW Nut Splitters provide safe and efficient removal of heavily corroded or seized nuts through controlled hydraulic splitting action. The precision-engineered blade design with calibrated depth control ensures damage-free bolt preservation while eliminating sparks and impact hazards. Featuring quick setup and versatile sizing options, these tools deliver rapid nut removal in even the most challenging industrial environments.

Features

  • Equipped with an RFID chip for easy identification, inventory management, and maintenance tracking.
  • Safe: No flames. No sparks. No risk to volatile surroundings. No hammers or impact are needed. No flying fragments.
  • Fast: Cuts a 3" nut in 20 to 30 seconds, as opposed to approx. 20 minutes with a hammer & chisel or torch. No damage to bolt or stud threads by calibrating the chisel to cut only the nut.
  • Easy setup: You can be up and away in less than 5 minutes.
  • Versatile: Nut splitters cut the hardest nuts, and the range of models compares very favorably to the competition.
  • Price: A competitive price for the unit plus savings that reduce downtime and prevent damage to costly bolts and studs.
