Torque Pump

TheGAW Torque Pumps are high-performance devices designed to generate pressures up to 10,000 PSI (700 bar), meeting the demands of rigorous bolting applications. Available in electrical and pneumatic drive systems, these pumps feature a two-stage design for faster cycle times and efficient tool operation. They ensure durability and ease of use in harsh industrial environments.
