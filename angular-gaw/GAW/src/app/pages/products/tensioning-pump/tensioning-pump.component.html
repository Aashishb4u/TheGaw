Tensioning Pump

TheGAW Tensioning Pumps deliver exceptional performance with pressure capabilities up to 2000 bar. Available in both air-driven and electric variants, these pumps feature a two-stage design that ensures rapid system filling and precise pressure control. The compact, robust construction with an integrated cooling system enables continuous operation in demanding environments.
